Darjeeling


Darjeeling, known as the "Queen of the Hills," is famous for its scenic tea gardens, panoramic views of the Himalayas, and the historic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  1. Darjeeling Himalayan Railway – Famous toy train offering scenic mountain rides.
  2. Tiger Hill – Stunning sunrise views over the Kanchenjunga peak.
  3. Batasia Loop – Spiral railway track with a war memorial and garden.
  4. Peace Pagoda – A serene Japanese-style pagoda with panoramic views.
  5. Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park – Home to snow leopards and Himalayan animals.
  • Thukpa – Tibetan noodle soup, perfect for chilly weather.
  • Momos – Steamed or fried dumplings with flavorful fillings.
  • Churpee – Traditional Himalayan cheese snack.
  • Darjeeling Tea – World-famous aromatic black tea.
  • Gundruk Soup – Fermented leafy green soup popular in the hills.
