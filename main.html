Darjeeling


Darjeeling is a beautiful hill station in India known for its tea gardens, cool climate, and scenic mountain views.

  1. Tiger Hill – sunrise view
  2. Batasia Loop – railway loop
  3. Darjeeling Himalayan Railway – toy train ride
  4. Peace Pagoda – calm place
  5. Tea Gardens – famous plantations
  • Momos – popular snack
  • Thukpa – noodle soup
  • Darjeeling Tea – famous tea
  • Sael Roti – traditional food
  • Gundruk – fermented dish
